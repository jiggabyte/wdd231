Site Plan: Heartland Harvest Cooperative.

Site Name

Heartland Harvest Cooperative.

The site name represents a farming cooperative society focused on sustainable agriculture and community farming. It conveys the idea of working together for the benefit of the farming community, which resonates with the core values of the cooperative.

Optional domain availability: heartland-coop.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Heartland Harvest Cooperative website is to serve as a central hub for cooperative members and aspiring farmers. The site will provide vital resources, farm locations, upcoming events, membership benefits, and real-time weather and crop forecast data to support sustainable agricultural practices.

Target Market

The site is for farmers and agro-related establishments operating Owerri and surroundings..

Site Goals

The site intents to increase interactivity and collaboration among farmers and also provide a front to assist local farmers and agro-related business to thrive.

User Personas

Small Farmer Owner: Chike is a 45-year-old farmer who owns a small banana plantation and would love to get tips and updates that will make his bananas disease free and increase his yield. Pesticide Dealers: Kelechi is a 35-year-old store owner is seeking for farmers to supply pesticides to and also is seeking for recommendations to know which particular pesticides to buy to be able to solve farmers problem and make better sales.

Scenarios

  • What are the sustainable farming techniques practiced by the cooperative's members?
  • How can I join the Heartland Harvest Cooperative, and what are the membership benefits?
